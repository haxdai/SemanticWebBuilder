INFOTEC WebBuilder 3 Main Information

 

 

INFOTEC WebBuilder 3X is a technological platform where the user can incorporate HTML and XML elements in a virtual space, in a really easy way according to your necessities and expectations to your web site.

 

 

INFOTEC WebBuilder 3X has grouped a different technological standards to build sites with a completely open structure and also counts with the possibility to integrate sites or systems developed with other technologies, different as it is, this technologies can use standards to generate recognition information. WebBuilder 3X counts with the advanced technology of Semantic Webs and also the regular options of WebBuilder Portal Manager
